Our Rooms

Hotel rooms available for rent cater to a wide range of preferences and needs, from basic accommodations to luxurious retreats. A standard room is a practical option, typically featuring a comfortable bed, a private bathroom, and essential amenities such as air conditioning, Wi-Fi, and a television, making it ideal for solo travelers or couples. For those seeking more comfort and style, the deluxe room offers additional space, premium furnishings, and sometimes a scenic view, complemented by upgraded amenities like a minibar and high-quality toiletries. Families often prefer the family room, designed with extra beds or bunk beds, ample seating areas, and kid-friendly features to ensure a pleasant stay. For a touch of luxury, the suite provides an expansive layout with separate living and sleeping areas, elegant decor, and exclusive services, perfect for extended stays or special occasions.
